Hi, I’m Tyler — ride‑share driver, aspiring behavior tech, and a relentless idea machine.

I like building things that make everyday life smoother: safer rides, calmer spaces, and simple tools that quietly do the work. My brain runs fast; structure is how I steer it. This site is where I park my projects so they don’t just live in my head.

I’m neurodivergent (ADHD/autism) and I design for brains like mine: clear labels, one idea per chunk, and easy off‑ramps when attention shifts. You’ll see big targets, clean spacing, and features like Dark Mode, Reduce Motion, and Focus Mode.

Community matters. From dog‑park culture to driver advocacy, I’m interested in systems that help people feel safe, respected, and included — especially folks who often get left out.

If you’re aligned with that, say hi. I’m always up for swapping notes, testing ideas, or building something useful together.
