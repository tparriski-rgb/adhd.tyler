Ideas

Welcome to my idea garden — an organised collection of brainstorms, half‑baked concepts and future experiments. Feel free to explore and get inspired.

Community Rebuilding

Sensory‑Friendly Community Hub

A network of safe, calming spaces where neurodivergent folks can gather, work, play and support one another. Features include sensory rooms, clear signage and friendly staff.

#community#accessibility

Microtransit Hubs

Pop‑up microtransit hubs bridging airports, downtowns and event venues with pre‑scheduled shuttle loops. Helps reduce isolation and offers predictable, affordable rides.

#mobility#events

After‑Hours Lounge

An invite‑only, mellow pop‑up lounge featuring board games, soft lighting and ND‑friendly zones. A space to socialise without the chaos of a bar scene.

#social#events

ND‑Friendly Media

ADHD Superhero Comic

A comic series starring an ADHD hero whose superpower is hyperfocus. Stories tackle distractions, resilience and friendship with humour and honesty.

#comics#representation

ND Life Hacks Podcast

A podcast sharing practical tips, Q&A and interviews around living well as a neurodivergent adult. Imagine “life design for different brains”.

#podcast#neurodivergent

Conversation Card Game

A deck of questions and prompts to help autistic and ADHD people practise social scripts, active listening and empathy. Designed for game nights or therapy sessions.

#game#social

Rides & Tools

Acceptance Rate Strategy

A rules‑light decision tree to help ride‑share drivers keep high acceptance while still cherry‑picking profitable trips. Includes thresholds for pay/mile, pay/minute and estimated hourly earnings.

#rideshare#toolkit

Respect Riders Sticker

Helmet graphic and slogan (“I Respect Riders”) to signal motorcycle allyship. Comes in matte and reflective options, and includes a QR code pledge page.

#merch#advocacy

Neurodivergent T‑Shirt Line

A series of affirming, witty T‑shirts featuring ADHD/autistic slogans in family‑friendly and NSFW variants. Built on a print‑on‑demand platform.

#merch#identity
