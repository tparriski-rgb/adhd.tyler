Ventures

This page is a map of my big ideas — from flagship businesses to advocacy campaigns, micro‑products and event concepts. Use the sections below to explore.

Flagship Ventures

Paws of Olympus / Athena’s Place

A membership indoor dog park and café built as a community hub, with tiered membership pricing (Zeus VIP at $199.99/month). Offers daycare, grooming, training, boarding and events with solar energy offsets and strict behavioural rules for a clean, safe environment.

Olympus Ride Services

A premium private driving brand that provides flat‑rate rides in my Highlander with consistent communication, small perks and no surprise fees. Includes deposit handling, confirmation scripts and a referral program.

Rideshare Tools & Advocacy

Android OCR App

On‑device OCR for Lyft screenshots on a Samsung S23 Ultra. Categorises and logs ride earnings offline with optional Drive backup. Revenues come from paid app tiers or consulting.

Universal Screenshot Analyzer

A desktop tool/CLI that watches a folder, OCRs receipts and statements and normalises them into a single dataset with a simple dashboard. Open‑core business model.

Multi‑Ride Earnings Calculator

An interactive calculator for inputting multiple rides with time, distance and bonuses to derive true profit metrics. Offers a freemium tier with pro analytics.

Acceptance‑Rate Strategy & Aid

A guide and printable decision aid to help drivers maintain ≥90% acceptance while cherry‑picking good rides. Uses simple thresholds and charts tied to Analyzer outputs.

How Lyft Nudges Drivers

A data campaign with an interactive deck, charts and a complaint outline demonstrating how driver apps manipulate behaviour (e.g., Turbo acceptance pressure). Intended for media and policymakers.

Auto & Merch

LED & Auto Mods Microbusiness

An installation and coaching service for ambient lighting, centralised LED control and third‑row charging ports in vehicles. Transparent pricing and photo portfolio.

Monster Pixel Packs & Pixel‑Art Library

A set of prebuilt message packs for driver LED signs (“U lift my ♥️”) and a pixel‑art prompt library for custom animations. Sold as digital downloads.

Respect Riders Sticker Line

A line of stickers with a helmet graphic and “I Respect Riders” to signal allyship to the motorcycle community. Comes in matte and reflective finishes.

Thank‑You & Perk Cards

Classy cards for passengers offering a perk (music control, ask me anything) and a soft reminder to rebook directly. Encourages tips and repeat bookings.

ND‑Themed T‑Shirt Line

An apparel line featuring smart and edgy neurodivergent slogans, available in family‑friendly and NSFW variants. Made via print‑on‑demand.

Content & Media

Driver–Passenger Q&A Series

An Instagram carousel and reel series showing both sides of common ride‑share interactions with commentary. Aims to build empathy and laughs.

Transit & Consulting

VIA Microtransit Partnership

A proposal to create a hub‑and‑spoke microtransit system connecting airports, downtowns and event centres with scheduled, dynamic routing.

Hops & Hounds Consulting

A consulting mini‑deck with high‑ROI tweaks for a competitor dog park (signage, QR prompts, breed nights), aiming to build goodwill and potential collaboration.

Event Marketing Services

Marketing packages for authors, musicians and local creators — including PDF one‑sheets, signage templates and content schedules.

Pop‑Up After‑Hours Lounge

An invite‑only, mellow lounge with music, board games, ND‑friendly zones and BYOB lean. Designed for adults seeking calm socialising.

Ghost Counter

A centralised counter consolidating fast‑food takeout from multiple brands in one location, reducing wait times and miles for drivers.

Cable‑Box Reinvention

Reimagining the set‑top box as a community hub displaying event notices, transit overlays, merchant promotions and neighbourhood alerts.
