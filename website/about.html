About Me

Hi, I'm Tyler – a rideshare driver, future behavioural technician and neurodivergent creator based in San Antonio, Texas. This space is my creative playground where I share ideas, projects and reflections on life with ADHD and autism.

My Story

By day I drive for Lyft, weaving through Austin and San Antonio, meeting hundreds of people each week. On the side I'm studying to become a registered behavioural technician. Losing access to Uber after a wrongful judgment taught me resilience; I turned adversity into opportunity by pursuing my passion for working with people and animals.

I'm proudly autistic and ADHD. My neurodivergence fuels my curiosity, creativity and sense of humour. Whether it's dreaming up a solar‑powered dog park, designing a removable sticker to make ridesharing safer or crafting self‑care systems, my brain loves to explore possibilities.

Interests & Skills

  • Entrepreneurship: Developing Athena’s Place – a membership‑based, indoor dog park and venue.
  • Gaming: Smite and FPS games – catch me strategising support roles or fine‑tuning my aim.
  • DIY Projects: Custom car lighting mods that make rides more fun and inviting.
  • Community Building: Creating inclusive spaces and neurodivergent‑friendly resources.
  • Food & Drink: Classic breakfasts and mixology – there’s nothing like a boozy brunch.
