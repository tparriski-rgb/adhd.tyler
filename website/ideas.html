Ideas & Concepts

This page collects my brain dumps, half‑formed schemes and future dreams. Feel free to explore and share feedback – these concepts are works in progress that may one day become full projects.

Community Rebuilding

  • Sensory‑Friendly Community Hub: A safe haven with quiet rooms, clear signage and calming colours, catering to neurodivergent patrons and families.
  • Mutual Aid Network: A local group where rideshare drivers, tech workers and creatives swap services and support each other.
  • Pop‑up Clean‑Up Events: Organising monthly neighbourhood clean‑ups with breakfast sandwiches and tunes to build camaraderie and improve public spaces.

ND‑Friendly Media

  • ADHD Superhero Comic: A web comic featuring a hero whose quick‑thinking brain becomes their greatest power and biggest challenge.
  • Podcast: Neurodivergent Notes: A casual chat show covering life hacks, gaming and community stories with humour and candour.
  • Interactive Blog: A space where readers can vote on which project I develop next or submit their own ideas for features at Athena’s Place.

Self‑Care & Communication Hacks

  • Colour‑Coded Planner System: A physical or digital planner using colours and stickers to prioritise tasks, appointments and rest for ADHD brains.
  • Conversation Card Game: A deck of prompt cards that help practise active listening, empathy and humour in social settings.
  • Empathy Timer App: A mobile app that reminds you to take breaks, drink water and send supportive messages to friends.
  • Mindful Morning Routine: A ritual combining classic breakfast, journalling and a favourite soundtrack to set the tone for the day.
