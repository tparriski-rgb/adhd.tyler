My Projects

Here’s a closer look at the bigger ideas I’m developing. These projects blend my love for animals, technology, community and personal growth.

Athena’s Place

Athena’s Place is my vision for a membership‑based indoor dog park that doubles as a community hub. Imagine a space where pups can run free, humans can enjoy craft drinks and classic brunch, and everyone feels at home. Key features include:

  • Climate‑controlled, solar‑powered indoor play area and dog daycare.
  • Grooming and spa services onsite for pampered pooches.
  • A full bar and café serving breakfast, lunch and boozy beverages.
  • Large projector and game nights for sporting events and watch parties.
  • Sensory‑friendly zones designed with ADHD/autistic guests in mind.

I’m currently researching locations in Austin and the surrounding area, exploring sustainable building options like solar panels and smart energy management.

DIY Car Lighting Mods

As a ride‑share driver, I like my vehicle to stand out and make passengers feel welcome. My DIY car lighting mods include:

  • Subtle accent lights under the dash and seats to create a cosy atmosphere.
  • Exterior courtesy lights to illuminate the ground when doors open.
  • A removable roof‑mounted sign to indicate ride availability.
  • Integration with music or ambient noise for dynamic effects.

I plan tutorials and posts explaining how to set up these mods safely and affordably (no expensive fibre optics needed!).

ND Self‑Care & Conversation Toolkit

Living with ADHD and autism means finding tools that work for your brain. I’m creating a toolkit of resources for neurodivergent folks, including:

  • Printable habit trackers and planners adapted for ADHD minds.
  • Conversation cards and prompts to practise social skills.
  • Guided self‑care routines with built‑in positive reinforcement.
  • Blog posts about managing executive function and sensory overload.

These resources will be available soon – my goal is to share strategies that make everyday life more manageable and fun.
